About Me

Hi there!

My name's Yigang Qin [ɪgʌn tʃɪŋ].
I'm an undergraduate computer science student at the City University of Hong Kong .
Luckily advised by Prof. Zhicong Lu.
Also getting a minor in Modern and Contemporary Chinese Literature.
I was born in Sichuan, China, and now live in Hong Kong.

My research interests broadly cover human-computer interaction (HCI), social computing, critical computing, and infrastructure studies. I'm particularly interested in studying how computational technologies impact the myriad aspects of our life and work, and the way they produce and reproduce inequality and power. I examine both virtual and material realms of computing, including infrastructures, data, and algorithms.

In my leisure time, I enjoy reading, movies, painting, rock music, traveling, and spicy food.
