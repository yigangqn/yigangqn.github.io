about me

Hi there!

My name's Yigang Qin.
Pronounce it /i:gun tfing/
I'm a computer science student.
I was born in China, but now live in Hong Kong.

My research interests are human-computer interaction (HCI), social computing, critical computing, and infrastructure studies. I'm particularly interested in studying how new information technologies impact our work, production, and social divisions. I aspire to examine both virtual and material realms of computing to understand information infrastructures, data, and algorithms.

In my leisure time, I enjoy reading, movies, arts, and traveling.
