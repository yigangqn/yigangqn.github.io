Welcome!

I'm a PhD student in Information Science and Technology at Syracuse University, advised by EunJeong Cheon. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong, advised by Zhicong Lu. I was born in Sichuan, China, spent four years in Hong Kong, and now live in Syracuse, USA.

My research falls in the fields of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Science and Technology Studies (STS). I draw on my training in sociology, anthropology, and informatics to study issues of labor and gender intersecting with digital technologies.
